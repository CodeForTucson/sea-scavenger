Welcome to Sea Scavenger!

In this game, you are part of the circular economy.

What is a circular economy? We're glad you asked!

In a circular economy, nothing goes to waste. Instead, when goods reach the end of their life, they are remanufactured into something new.

Your task in this game is to recover plastic waste from the coasts of countries around the world so the items can be remanufactured.

  • For each turn, you will be given a mission to recover a specific type of plastic waste.
  • You then choose the country your team will recover the plastic from to achieve the mission.
  • If there is enough of that type of plastic waste at that location, you will succeed and get a new mission.
  • If you don't retrieve enough waste, the game is over.

Good luck!
